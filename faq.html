Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Cold Pressed" or "Wood Pressed" mean?

Cold pressing (Mara Chekku) is a traditional Indian method of oil extraction where seeds are crushed using a wooden pestle. This creates immense mechanical friction without generating external heat. By keeping temperatures naturally low, the oil retains its original antioxidants, vitamins, and bold flavor, unlike factory refined oils which are stripped using extreme heat and chemicals.

Are your oils organic?

While we do not carry mass-commercial organic certifications, we source 100% of our seeds directly from trusted local farmers in Tamil Nadu who employ natural, pesticide-free farming methods.

How should I store the oil?

To preserve maximum freshness, store our oils in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Ensure the cap is tightly sealed to avoid oxidation.

Do you add preservatives to increase shelf life?

Absolutely not. SouthGold Pressings produces 100% pure, unadulterated oil. There are zero additives, argemone oil mixes, or artificial preservatives present in our products.
