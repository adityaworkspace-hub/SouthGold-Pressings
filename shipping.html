Shipping & Delivery

1. Processing Time

Due to the artisanal nature of our Mara Chekku wood-pressing method, inventory is pressed freshly in small batches. All orders are processed and dispatched within 2 to 3 business days from our extraction facility in Tamil Nadu.

2. Domestic Shipping Rates

We provide reliable shipping across India. Rates are calculated based on the weight of the oils and your delivery location.

  • Free Shipping on all domestic orders exceeding ₹1,500.
  • Standard flat-rate shipping of ₹80 applies for orders under the threshold limit.

3. Delivery Timelines

Once dispatched, standard delivery times are generally:

  • South India: 2 - 4 business days
  • Rest of India: 5 - 7 business days

4. Packaging Standards

To ensure our cold-pressed oils reach you safely and without oxidation, we utilize premium protective glass & heavy-duty PET packaging. Each item is strictly seal-checked prior to dispatch.

5. Track Your Order

Upon dispatch, an automated email containing your courier tracking link will be sent to your registered address. For urgent tracking concerns or missed deliveries, please reach out via WhatsApp at +91 96007 81450.
