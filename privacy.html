Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

At SouthGold Pressings, we collect essential personal information when you process an order or contact us via our forms. This includes:

  • Name and demographic information.
  • Contact details such as email address (aditya.2301005@srec.ac.in) and phone numbers.
  • Shipping and billing addresses.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information primarily to:

  • Process, fulfill, and correctly ship your cold-pressed oil orders.
  • Communicate tracking and customer support updates regarding your purchase.
  • Enhance our website's functionality and user experience based on internal analytics.

3. Data Protection and Security

We take the security of your personal information very seriously. Payment processing is handled by third-party encrypted gateways, and SouthGold Pressings does not store or maintain your sensitive credit card data on our servers.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We merely share necessary operational information with trusted shipping couriers and payment processors to complete your transactions.
