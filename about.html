The Process

Gentle Wood Pressing

Modern refined oils are subjected to intense heat and harsh chemicals, stripping away the natural goodness.

At SouthGold Pressings, we use the ancestral Mara Chekku method. Heavy wooden pestles crush the seeds at a very low RPM. This ensures the temperature remains completely cool, acting as a natural safeguard for vital nutrients.

  • ✓ Zero heat generation
  • ✓ Preserves Vitamin E and Antioxidants
  • ✓ Authentic natural aroma
